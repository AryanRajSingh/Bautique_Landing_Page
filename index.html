The Brand

Sherri Hill’s exclusive dress collections epitomize the fashionable lifestyle of today’s trendsetting women. Each dress is designed to enhance and fit the female form with fashion-forward styling and wearability. Her design career began to gain recognition when various Miss USA, Miss Universe and Miss America contestants won titles in her gowns. Now, the Hill family business has a network of over 1,000 stores selling the brand in over 52 countries. In 2017 Sherri Hill headquarters moved to a sleek space in Austin, Texas. The Hill’s also maintain a dress design studio and showroom in Atlanta
